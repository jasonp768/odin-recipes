Thanksgiving Stuffing

Description:

When Thanksgiving comes around, theres only one dish that I look forward to more than the turkey. That's the Thanksgiving Stuffing. With its outer crunchy texture and soft chewy insides it is truly the most delicious, balanced meal I can think of making for this special evening. It has an extremely buttery, herby flavor that blends well with all the other fixtures expected on a Thanksgiving plate.

Although most people would call this dish "stuffing" as it is commonly used to stuff the turkey before cooking. In my opinion, it is best served as a side item. And lets be honest, Thanksgiving is ALL about the side items! This recipe feeds about 8 people but can be scaled up or down accordingly. If your family is anything like mine, the stuffing is the first to go, so the more the better! I hope your family loves this classic meal as much as mine does!

HAPPY THANKSGIVING!
