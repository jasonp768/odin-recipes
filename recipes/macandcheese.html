Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Description:

Everybody loves macaroni and cheese but we all know there is a difference between regular macaroni and cheese and special occasion macaroni and cheese. For thanksgiving, it just seems to hit a little different. It is usually a recipe that has been passed down from generation to generation seemingly getting better with each addition. There truly is no wrong way to create this dish.

To some it is considered the most universal comfort food with many different variations. With its crunchy outer layer and gooey center it creates the perfect mixture for a tasty dish. Any cheese will work but my personal preference is extra-sharp cheddar cheese and colby jack. This is my second favorite dish to cook for any special occasion, especially Thanksgiving. Enjoy!
