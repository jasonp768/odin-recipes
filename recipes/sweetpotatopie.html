Sweet Potato Pie

Description:

Being from the south, there is one dish that will always be on the table for any special occasion. That dish is the Sweet Potato Pie. A delicious southern favorite, sweet potato pie is simple, creamy and the perfect complimentary dessert to a Thanksgiving meal. Another recipe that is generally passed down from generation to generation, this is a family favorite.

With very few ingredients, the flavor comes mostly from the sweet potatoes themselves and the crust of the pie. This is a simple pie that is a great addition to any special occasion, especially Thanksgiving. So, for my last recipe for this project, please enjoy the Southern Sweet Potato Pie!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 unbaked pie crust (9 inch)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring pot of water to a boil
  3. Boil sweet potatoes whole with skin for 40 to 50 minutes
  4. Remove from pot and rinse with cold water, removing skin
  5. Use hands to break apart sweet potato into a large bowl
  6. Add butter and mix well
  7. Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon, and vanilla extract
  8. Beat together until mixture is smooth
  9. Pour the filling into the pie crust
  10. Bake in oven for 55 to 60 minutes
  11. Use knife to check if fully cooked (knife should come out of pie clean)
  12. Enjoy!
